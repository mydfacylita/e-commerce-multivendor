## 1. Introduction

MYD Facilyta Technology ("we", "our", or "the Company"), operator of the MYDSHOP platform, is committed to protecting the personal information of all users, sellers, and partners who interact with our services, including our integration with TikTok Shop Open Platform.

This Personal Information Protection Policy describes how we collect, use, store, share, and protect personal data in compliance with applicable data protection laws, including Brazil's Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados (LGPD – Law No. 13.709/2018) and internationally recognized data protection standards.

---

## 2. Data We Collect

In the context of our TikTok Shop integration, we collect and process the following categories of data:

| Category | Data Types |
|---|---|
| Account Information | Full name, email address, phone number |
| Business Information | Store name, CNPJ/CPF, company address |
| Authentication Data | OAuth tokens (access token, refresh token) |
| Product Data | Product titles, descriptions, prices, images, stock levels |
| Order Data | Order IDs, item details, shipping information, order status |
| Shop Data | Shop ID, shop name, region, seller status |

We do **not** collect payment card numbers, passwords, or sensitive financial data.

---

## 3. Purpose of Data Collection

Personal and business data is collected solely for the following purposes:

- **Platform Integration:** To synchronize product listings, orders, and inventory between MYDSHOP and TikTok Shop on behalf of the seller.
- **Account Management:** To authenticate sellers and manage their connected marketplace accounts.
- **Order Processing:** To display and manage TikTok Shop orders within the MYDSHOP ERP dashboard.
- **Service Improvement:** To monitor integration performance and resolve technical issues.

We do **not** sell, rent, or trade personal data to third parties for commercial purposes.

---

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process personal data based on the following legal grounds:

- **Contractual Necessity:** Processing is required to provide the services agreed upon with the seller.
- **Legitimate Interest:** To maintain the security and integrity of our platform.
- **Consent:** Where required, we obtain explicit consent from users before processing their data.
- **Legal Obligation:** To comply with applicable laws and regulatory requirements in Brazil.

---

## 5. Data Sharing

We may share data with the following parties, strictly for operational purposes:

- **TikTok Shop Open Platform:** To execute API calls for product and order management on behalf of the seller.
- **Cloud Infrastructure Providers:** Our platform is hosted on secure cloud servers. Providers are contractually bound to protect data.
- **Legal Authorities:** Only when required by law or valid legal order.

We require all third-party service providers to maintain appropriate data protection standards.

---

## 6. Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored on encrypted servers located in Brazil or within jurisdictions with adequate data protection standards.
- Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel only, on a need-to-know basis.
- OAuth tokens are stored encrypted in our database using industry-standard encryption.
- We implement HTTPS/TLS for all data transmission.
- We conduct regular security reviews and access audits.
- In the event of a data breach, we will notify affected users and relevant authorities within the timeframes required by law.

---

## 7. Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|---|---|
| OAuth Tokens | Until the seller disconnects the integration or token expires |
| Order Data | 5 years (as required by Brazilian tax law) |
| Account Data | Duration of the service agreement + 1 year |
| Log Data | 6 months |

Upon request, we will delete personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

---

## 8. User Rights

Under LGPD and applicable international standards, users have the right to:

- **Access** — Request a copy of their personal data we hold.
- **Correction** — Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
- **Deletion** — Request deletion of personal data (right to erasure).
- **Portability** — Request transfer of data to another service provider.
- **Restriction** — Request limitation of processing in certain circumstances.
- **Objection** — Object to processing based on legitimate interests.
- **Withdrawal of Consent** — Withdraw consent at any time without affecting prior processing.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact: **contato@mydsistemas.com.br**

---

## 9. Cross-Border Data Transfers

Where data is transferred outside Brazil (e.g., to TikTok's servers), we ensure that such transfers comply with LGPD requirements, including reliance on standard contractual clauses or adequacy decisions where applicable.
